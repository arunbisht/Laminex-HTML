Laminex Metaline is an innovative, easy to install aluminium-based splashback with a clean continuous glass-like look, but with superior cost efficiency and versatility. Unlike glass, Metaline is designed to be easily bent or curved for seamless corners and edges. Metaline can also be used in a range of commercial and residential applications such as bathroom wall linings, feature walls and curved pillars.
