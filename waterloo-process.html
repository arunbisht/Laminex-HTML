Wet and Dry Areas

Laminex Multipurpose Compact Laminate 13mm

  • Self-supporting high pressure laminate based on thermosetting resins homogeneously reinforced with cellulose fibres.
  • High pressure decorative laminate applied to both sides.
  • Greenguard certified.
  • Can be easily wiped down.
  • All cut edges have a polished black edge that doesn’t require additional laminate.
  • Excellent resistance to scratches, graffiti, vandalism and water.
  • Premium level of hygiene protection in healthcare environments.

Dry Areas Only

Laminex Structural MR E0 MDF 18mm/32mm

  • Moisture resistant medium density fibreboard.
  • Decorative melamine surface pressed on both sides of the board.
  • Superior impact resistance together with enhanced wear and abrasion properties.
  • Global GreenTag Certified- Level A.
  • Recommended for low abuse areas such as toilet blocks.
